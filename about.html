About Derive

Derive was created to be an interactive, easy-to-use resource for learning and strengthening skills in Calculus. The Derive Team is a group of three high school students at High Technology High School in Lincroft, New Jersey. Our mission is to make interactive learning resources available to help people like you learn Calculus by experiencing, experimenting, and enjoying Calculus as a subject. If you have any comments or feedback, you can email us at anmascillaro@ctemc.org or leave us a message below. Have fun and explore the world of math!
